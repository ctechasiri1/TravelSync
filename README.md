# ✈️ TravelSync

![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/Platform-iOS-blue.svg)
![Language](https://img.shields.io/badge/Language-Swift-orange.svg)

**TravelSync** is an iOS application designed to help users seamlessly plan, coordinate, and synchronize their travel itineraries. Whether you're traveling solo or coordinating a group trip, TravelSync keeps all your travel details organized in one place.

## ✨ Features

* **Itinerary Management:** Easily add flights, hotels, car rentals, and activities to your trip.
* **Group Syncing:** Collaborate with friends and family in real-time. Share itineraries and update plans together. *(Placeholder for potential features)*
* **Offline Access:** Access your travel plans even when you don't have an internet connection.
* **Push Notifications:** Get timely reminders for flight check-ins, reservations, and itinerary changes.

## 🛠 Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have met the following requirements:
* Mac running macOS 13.0 or later.
* **Xcode 15.0** or later.
* **iOS 16.0+** deployment target.
* (Optional) CocoaPods or Swift Package Manager (SPM) for dependency management.
